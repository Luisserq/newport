PROFESSIONAL SKILLS

My Expertise.

Research

Experienced with conducting interviews with users, asking open ended questions and taking notes. Created multiple surveys for case studies to gather quantitative data.

Prototyping

Generated prototypes and documented the process of the products' iterations through recordings of interviews, usability tests, and documentation of the product at different stages of design.

Empathy

Stimulated user frustrations and problems through empathizing with them via interviews and user feedback. Allowing me to create empathy maps and user journey maps to further empathize with my user, finding potential solutions.

Information Architecture

Amplify the users' understanding of the website using a design that places the most important information/call to actions on the screen so that they are emphasized.

Collaboration

Expanded my experience with collaborating through past jobs and recent projects dealing with UX design. I love working with others and would describe myself as adaptable and great at dealing with conflict that may arise in a group setting.

CASE STUDIES

My Projects.

Pawpup

Created a functional mobile app that caters to dog owners who want to socialize their dogs more and have puppy playdates with 3 group members. Assembled a user flow, empathy map, competitor analysis, conducted usability tests/interviews after iterating on the prototype.

Fibrarian

With 2 group members, prototyped a mobile app that allows users to trade or donate books they no longer want, building a community of book readers that seek new reading experiences. We all developed wireframes and prototypes so the app was clickable and fully functional while formulating an empathy map, a competitor analysis, a user persona, etc.

BLS.Gov Redesign

Redesigned the website of a local non-profit with 3 group members. Was in charge of the competitor analysis and work was evenly distributed throughout the members. Such as interviews, iterating on prototypes, an affinity diagram, etc.

HASB Redesign

Redesigned the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics’ government website so that it is less dense with information and that the user wouldn’t waste time being stunned by the abundance of options. Executed the essential UX Design process of conducting one-on-one interviews, sending out a survey, going usability tests, creating a user persona, etc.
